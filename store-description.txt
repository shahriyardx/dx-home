Transform your new tab into a clean, minimal, and functional workspace with DxHome. Designed for productivity, DxHome replaces the blank new tab page with a beautiful dashboard that keeps everything you need at your fingertips.

Key Features:

- Minimal & Clean Design: Clutter-free interface with a focus on simplicity. Every element is carefully placed to feel spacious yet functional.

- Custom Backgrounds: Pick from stunning preset wallpapers directly from the sidepanel, or right-click any image on the web to set it as your background.

- Smart Search Bar: Search the web right from your new tab page.

- Bookmarks: Your top sites displayed as clean shortcuts for one-click access.

- Tasks: Built-in todo list with deadlines, reminders, and notifications. Right-click any text or link to quickly create a task.

- Reading List: Save pages to read later from the sidepanel or right-click context menu. Mark as read, filter by status, and access from both new tab and sidepanel.

- Recent Tabs: Automatically tracks your recently closed tabs so you never lose one.

- Clock: Clean time display on the new tab page.

- Sidepanel: Open the sidepanel from the extension icon to manage backgrounds, reading list, and settings.

- Settings: Toggle visibility of every section (clock, search, bookmarks, recent tabs, tasks, reading list) to customize exactly what you see.

DxHome is lightweight, fast, and built for users who want a minimal, aesthetic, and productive new tab experience.
